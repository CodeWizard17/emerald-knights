About Us

We are a student-led non-profit dedicated to promoting the game of chess, fostering strategic thinking, and building a vibrant community of chess enthusiasts.

Our Founding

The Emerald Knights Chess Club was founded on October 10, 2024, by Advay Bansal with a mission to empower youth through chess.

Our Mission

To provide a supportive and engaging environment for players of all levels to learn, grow, and compete while promoting academic excellence, patience, and sportsmanship.
