About Us

Welcome to the Emerald Knights Chess Club! We are dedicated to promoting the game of chess, fostering strategic thinking, and building a community of chess enthusiasts.

Our Founding

The Emerald Knights Chess Club is a non-profit organization founded by Advay Bansal, on October 10, 2024.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a supportive and engaging environment for chess players to improve their skills, participate in tournaments, and develop a lifelong love for chess.
